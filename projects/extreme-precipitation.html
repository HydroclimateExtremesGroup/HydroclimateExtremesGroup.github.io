Extreme Precipitation Research

Our research focuses on the measurement and prediction of extreme rainfall at high resolution using multiple data sources including rain gauges, weather radar, satellites, and weather and climate models.

Research Focus Areas

  • High-resolution rainfall measurement techniques
  • Integration of multiple rainfall data sources
  • Extreme rainfall prediction methods
  • Weather radar applications in hydrology
  • Satellite precipitation product development

Current Projects

Radar-Based Rainfall Analysis

Development of methods to improve radar-based rainfall estimates for hydrologic applications.

Satellite Precipitation Uncertainty

Quantification and modeling of uncertainties in satellite-based precipitation estimates.

Multi-Source Data Integration

Methods for combining rainfall information from multiple measurement sources.
