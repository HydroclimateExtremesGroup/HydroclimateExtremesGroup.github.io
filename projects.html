Projects and Software

The Wisconsin Rainfall Project

Present day and future extreme rainfall statistics for Wisconsin, from the Hydroclimate Extremes Research Group and the Wisconsin Initiative on Climate Change Impacts

RainyDay

Open-source software for generating realistic extreme rainfall scenarios using stochastic storm transposition, supporting flood modeling in small and medium-sized watersheds

StormLab

Stochastic rainfall generator that simulates high-resolution rainfall fields over large river basins, incorporating climate model predictions for future flood risk assessment

STREAM

Space-Time Rainfall Error and Autocorrelation Model: Efficient framework for modeling space-time correlation structure of precipitation fields and satellite rainfall uncertainty
University of Wisconsin-Madison Civil and Environmental Engineering
